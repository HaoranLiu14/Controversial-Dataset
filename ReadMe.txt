Paper: Is your summary believable? Deep Context Understanding for Bug Report Summarization
This dataset consist of 39 annotated bug reports and 62 controversial sentences.
Sentence.csv: Sentences in bug reports and their sentence numbers.
Summary.csv: Summaries of bug reports and the sentence number of the sentences from the original report that gave the annotators the most information when writing the summary.
Controversial Sentence.csv: Controversial sentences in bug reports.
